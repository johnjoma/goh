AppSheet FAQs

Duplicate records on the appsheet.

step1

Try to find out what might be the cause of the duplicate records, i.e different phone numbers.

Step2

Find the person who registered the customer and find more details about the customer.

Step3

Send an email to It.support @suncuture.com for it to be sorted.

My appsheet does not sync.

step1

Clear cache and try to sync the appsheet again.

Step2

Check on your Network and restart your laptop

step3

If the above steps does not sort the issue,Screenshot the error, and send it to IT.SUPPORT@SUNCUTURE.COM for the issue to be sorted.

User profile cannot load.

NB, This is normally caused by slow Network

step1

Check your Network Connection, preferably if using bundles change to WIFI.

Step2

Clear all browsing data/cache.

Step3

Restart your laptop/phone.

Step4

Try login in again,and if the issue persist,Kindly send an email to it.support@sunculture.com

I cannot dispatch from the Appsheet.

3CX FAQs

What should I do if I am experiencing silent calls and customer can't hear me.

step1

Check if your audio is working on the device whether phone or PC your using by playing video on youtube or any other source .If working proceed to the next step.Check whether your internet is stable.Also try switching Network, preferably use Phone internet incase you were connected to wifi.Try accessing 3cx through your phone in case you are currently using a laptop, if it works then the issue is with the hardware being used.Report that to IT.Switch to Firefox Mozilla, Chrome usually has issues with 3cx on outbound calls.

Step2

Try changing the position, i.e try to find a place where the Network is strong and better.

Step3

Clear cache and restart your laptop.

Step4

If the above steps fails to solve the issue, report the issue to IT.SUPPORT@SUNCULTURE.COM.

My 3cx keeps disconnecting, what now?

Step 1

Do a simple test at www.speedtest.net and verify if the internet your getting is what you paid for from your provider

Step 2

Check if there is any device Tabs overloading your home network.

Step 3

If the issue persists reach out to IT support.

What should I do when 3cx says IP blacklisted?

Reach out to the IT to have your IP whitelisted.
